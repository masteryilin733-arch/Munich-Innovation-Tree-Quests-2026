🧭 The System

Five steps from city priority
to actionable insight

A complete loop: city defines what matters, AI makes it a game, students collect the data, AI validates it, and the city receives a live dashboard.

1
🏛
City defines priorities
Grünflächenamt uploads inspection targets, flagged zones and seasonal priorities to the dashboard.
2
🤖
AI converts into missions
Language model translates technical priorities into gamified student missions with rewards and clear objectives.
3
📷
Students collect data
Students photograph trees in the field. AI guides them live — species ID, condition check, geo-tagging.
4
✅
AI + peers validate
Each submission is cross-checked by AI and optionally peer-reviewed, building a reliability score.
5
📊
City receives insights
Live GIS feed, exportable GeoJSON, disease cluster alerts and health trend reports — updated in real time.
